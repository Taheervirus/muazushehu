Abou Me

Engr. Muazu Shehu Alkanchi is a dedicated professional in the field of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, currently serving as a Senior Training Officer at the Petroleum Training Institute, Delta State. Originally from Sokoto, he has built a strong academic and professional foundation, obtaining a Technical Certificate (NABTEB) and an NCE Technical from SSCOE Sokoto. He later earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Federal University of Technology Minna.

With extensive experience in both private and public sectors at the state and federal levels, Engr. Alkanchi specializes in electrical power operations and protection, substation design, energy management, and electrical machines. His hands-on expertise enables him to train and mentor students in various engineering courses, including Electrical Design & Drafting, Electrical Power Systems, and Electrical Machines.

He is an active participant in professional development, attending numerous conferences and seminars. His professional affiliations include:
  • Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN) – Registered & Licensed Engineer
  • Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE) – Member
  • Chartered Institution of Power Engineers of Nigeria (CIPEN) – Member
  • Nigerian Institution of Electrical & Electronics Engineers (NIEEE) – Member
  • Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE) – Member
  • Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria (TRCN) – Registered Teacher
